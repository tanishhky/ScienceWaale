Our Founder

I don't target to be a full time educator but still, for once I wanna complete the course because the time I was in 12th, I realised that we are already having abundant amount of content on various platforms, yet not everyone is able to understand. Students take hours seeing a lecture but still.. What's the use if they don't happen to understand it? And how useful are the revision notes if they happen to contain 30-60 pages atleast."

Keeping this in mind we are making videos according to how a teenage mind memorizes what he/she has seen and keeping in mind, what problems we faced in our years. The notes will be the shortest, yet easy to decipher what's mentioned.
